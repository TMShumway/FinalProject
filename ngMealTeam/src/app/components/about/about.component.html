About meal team6

Where elite foodies and out-of-idea meal preppers go to find great recipes and gastronomic inspiration



Similar to Strava or Runkeeper, Meal team6ers post their gastronomic experiences, contribute personal recipes to the community and socialize


Comments, ratings, friends, posts and recipes converge to feed the interests and palates of everyone.


  • Add recipes from the community to your personal cookbook
  • Contribute personal recipes to the community
  • View and rate recipes
  • View and comment on others' recipes and posts
  • Search for recipe ideas within the community
